About Us

At Eshaan Gill's Pizzeria we make the best tasting pizza for low prices. Our first priority is our customers and we will do whatever it takes to make our food better tasting and make the environment more enjoyable.We have 10 "80" inch T.V.'s where our customers get to choose what they would like to watch, for example if you want to watch a basketball game you can let us know and we will change the channel for what our customer's like.We opened up this restaraunt becuase our head chef, Eshaan Gill loves making pizza and it ran through his family and he decided to continue the family tradition and make pizza for a living. Eshaan loves making people happy and he is great at making people happy while eating his pizza. We never cheat our customers and spend a lot of time making fresh food everyday for our customers, Where Our Pizza Is Out of This World!
